About Me

I'm an experienced IT professional with a background in Web Development and SQL Administration. My journey in technology began with a passion for solving problems and building innovative solutions.

Driven by a commitment to continuous learning, I recently completed a full-stack boot camp to stay current with evolving technologies. Through immersive ongoing projects and collaboration with peers, I am solidifying my proficiency in modern web development frameworks and methodologies.

My journey is not just about achieving professional success, but also about personal growth and development. I thrive on challenges. In 2021, I participated in my first sprint triathlon without knowing how to swim! Although I was afraid, I did not let fear stop me. I jumped into the lake and swam the 1/3 mile. After not drowning or DNF'ing, I got the fever to keep going and training. I am currently prepping for an Iron Man 70.3 in 2025.

I'm also dedicated to giving back to my community by volunteering at local charities and organizations. Most recently I've volunteered to assist 2 small business owners with their websites. When I'm not working or volunteering, you can find me enjoying quality time with family and friends.
