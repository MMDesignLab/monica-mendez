Content Strategy

My professional portfolio website will be 1-page and consist of the following items.

Welcome

I will have 5 images that expand across the top of my page. These images will encompass who I am as a person sharing my professional goals and hobbies.

About Me

The about me section will list my professional work history, college degree, and newly acquired skillset bulletizing the new languages I was exposed to and have become proficient in during the 10-week full-stack boot camp.

Portfolio

I will create a 4-6 image gallery of items that, upon clicking, will open in a window with a quick synopsis of what the project was, what and how much I contributed, and things I would've done differently and if possible a link to the website. I want to showcase my ability to meet the client's needs, as well as express my ability to come up with fresh and new ideas.

Contact

A simple web form with textboxes for their name, phone number, email, and message that will be emailed to me when someone enters their information. I will also provide a link using my icons to my GitHub repository and LinkedIn profile in the nav or footer of my page.

Wireframes

My webpage will be formatted in a z format and be responsive for mobile view. I will create my wireframe on wireframe, visaliy or balsamiq.
