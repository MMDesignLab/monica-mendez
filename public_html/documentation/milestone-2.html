Content Strategy

My professional portfolio website will be one page and consist of the following items.

Welcome

I will have one background image with a stack of photos layered across the top of my page. These images will encompass who I am as a person sharing my professional goals and personal life. It will also state my name and position desired

About Me

The about me section will list my professional work history, college degree, and newly acquired skillset I was exposed to and have become proficient in during the 10-week full-stack boot camp.

Portfolio

I will create a 4-6 expanded card image gallery of items that, upon clicking, will expand with a quick synopsis of what the project was, what and how much I contributed, and things I would've done differently and if possible a link to the website. I want to showcase my ability to meet the client's needs, as well as express my ability to come up with fresh and new ideas.

Contact

A simple web form with textboxes for their name, phone number, email, and message that will be emailed to me when someone enters their information. I will also provide a link using icons to my GitHub repository and LinkedIn profile in the nav or footer of my page.

Wireframes

My webpage will be formatted in a z-format and be responsive for mobile view. I will create my wireframe on Balsamiq.com.
